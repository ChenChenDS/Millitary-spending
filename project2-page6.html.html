Summary

  • Globalization leads to a persistent increase in world immigration.

  • Countries received many immigrants for economic reasons, they think that immigrant can prop up the economy, and people move to other countries in order to get jobs.

  • Most of immigrants try to move to a better-developed country that is close to their origin country.

  • The refugee is important in explaining population movement. The country that welcomes immigrants tends to accept more refugees.
